IMAXX TV - Parenting Control

Keep Your Family's Viewing Experience Safe with IMAXX TV

At IMAXX TV, we understand that as a parent, you want to ensure that your children enjoy a safe and secure streaming experience. That's why we've developed Parenting Control, a set of tools that allow you to manage the content your children can access, keeping their entertainment both fun and appropriate for their age.

Key Features of Parenting Control:

  1. Customizable Age Restrictions: Set viewing restrictions based on the age of your child. You can limit content to specific ratings such as General, PG, or Teen, ensuring they only have access to age- appropriate shows and movies.
  2. Profile Creation for Kids: Create separate profiles for each child with tailored content suggestions. You can assign a profile to the "Kids Mode" to show only child-friendly content curated by IMAXX TV.
  3. Screen Time Management: Set daily or weekly time limits for how long your child can watch content on IMAXX TV. This feature helps you control screen time while ensuring they balance entertainment with other activities.
  4. Content Filtering and Blocking: Use the content filter to block specific genres or titles that you feel are inappropriate. You can also review and approve what your child wants to watch before they start.
  5. Secure PIN Protection: Lock adult or restricted content behind a PIN code, so you control access to sensitive materials.

How to Set Up Parenting Control:

  1. Log into your IMAXX TV account.
  2. Navigate to Settings and select Parenting Control.
  3. Create or update a profile for your child.
  4. Adjust the content filters, set screen time limits, and enable PIN protection.
  5. Save your settings, and enjoy peace of mind knowing your child's viewing experience is safeguarded.

IMAXX TV PARENTING CONTROL

Empower Safe Streaming for Kids

At IMAXX TV, your family’s viewing safety is our top priority. Our Parenting Control tools help you manage what your kids watch, when they watch it, and how long they spend streaming. It’s a smart, simple way to ensure they enjoy safe and age-appropriate content.

Features You’ll Love:

  1. Age-Based Restrictions: Filter content by age group – General, PG, Teen, and more – to control what shows and movies are visible to your child.
  2. Kids Profiles: Create custom profiles with Kids Mode enabled, showcasing only child-safe content reviewed and curated by our team.
  3. Screen Time Control: Set daily or weekly time limits to promote healthy viewing habits and balance screen time with other activities.
  4. Content Filtering: Block specific genres or titles, or allow access only after your approval, giving you total command of the watchlist.
  5. PIN Protection: Secure restricted or adult content behind a PIN so only you can access or modify settings.

How To Set It Up

  • Login to your IMAXX TV account.
  • Go to Settings > Parenting Control.
  • Create or update your child’s profile.
  • Set restrictions, enable Kids Mode, and add a secure PIN.
  • Relax while your kids enjoy safe content!

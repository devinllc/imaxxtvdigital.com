Imaxx TV Producers Partners Programme Overview-

The Imaxx TV Producers Partners Programme is designed to foster collaboration between content creators (filmmakers, producers, and distributors) and the Imaxx TV platform. This program provides content creators with the opportunity to distribute their content to a wider audience and gain access to a variety of distribution channels.

Key Features of the Programme:

1.Content Distribution: As a partner, your content will be showcased on Imaxx TV, giving you access to a global viewership. This provides filmmakers and producers the opportunity to reach a broader audience and increase their visibility.
2. Revenue Sharing: Typically, content distribution platforms like Imaxx TV offer a revenue-sharing model. Filmmakers and producers can expect to earn revenue based on the number of views or engagements their content generates on the platform.
3.Marketing and Promotion: The Producers Partners Programme may include promotional opportunities, helping you market your content to a larger audience. This could include social media promotion, featured content slots, or other marketing efforts by Imaxx TV.
4.Access to Analytics: Partners in the programme are likely to receive detailed analytics about how their content is performing. This information helps content creators refine their strategy and improve engagement.
5.Collaboration Opportunities: Imaxx TV may also offer collaboration opportunities with other creators, as well as access to industry professionals. These collaborations can enhance content quality and provide networking opportunities.

Guidelines for Participation:

1. Content Quality Standards: Content submitted must adhere to Imaxx TV's quality standards, ensuring it meets technical requirements like resolution, sound quality, and other production values.
2. Content Licensing: Filmmakers, producers, and distributors need to ensure they have the proper licensing for all elements of their content. This includes music, trademarks, and any third-party materials used.
3.Original Content: The programme likely prioritizes original content or content that offers unique value. Be sure to clarify whether your content is licensed for distribution or if you have the rights to distribute it.
4. Content Categorization: Ensure your content is properly categorized based on Imaxx TV's genre requirements. This ensures the right audience finds and engages with your work.
5.Compliance with Legal and Ethical Standards: All content should adhere to local laws, including copyright laws and broadcasting regulations. In addition, content must be free of any offensive, discriminatory, or harmful material.
6.Timely Submission: Partners should be timely with content submission and adhere to Imaxx TV's deadlines for uploading content or new episodes.
7.Marketing Commitment: Some platforms may request that producers commit to certain marketing efforts, such as promoting their content via social media or attending interviews and events.

How to Join the Programme:

*Submit an Application: Typically, filmmakers, producers, or content distributors must submit an application or pitch, which includes information about the content they want to distribute.
*Review Process: Imaxx TV will likely review the submitted content or project pitch to ensure it aligns with their vision and programming guidelines.
*Sign an Agreement: Once accepted into the programme, partners will be required to sign a distribution agreement that outlines the terms, conditions, and revenue-sharing structure.

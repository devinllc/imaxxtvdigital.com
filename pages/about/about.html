ABOUT US

Imaxx Tv Digital Private Limited also known as Imaxx Tv Is India’s First Hybrid OTT Platform, which runs on AVOD SVOD & TVOD format, providing wide variety of content from around the world. Imaxx tv was established in September 2023 in Mumbai with a vision of providing pocket entertainment for all age groups.


Imaxx TV is free streaming service and launched around the globe that allows our members to watch web series,movies on an internet connected device. Here we give platform to filmmakers, creators to upload content, films, shortfilms, webseries, shows etc on AVOD | SVOD | TVOD, is any form of SUBSCRIPTION module, such as videos that has been created as per the guidelines given by Imaxx Tv .You can download imaxx Tv for series, movies & Shows to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch it. We also acquire content in various languages with regional subtitles and English subtitles.


Our Vision: to provide entertainment and cinematic films to its viewers from around the world, at pocket friendly pricing and subscriptions.
